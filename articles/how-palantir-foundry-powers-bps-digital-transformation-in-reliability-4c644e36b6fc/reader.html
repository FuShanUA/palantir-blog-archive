How Palantir Foundry Powers bp’s Digital Transformation in Reliability

2022-03-22 · Original →

An exploration of how Palantir’s platform improves bp’s production and operations reliability, with reflections and lessons learned by bpSolutions Global Reliability Advisor for Data, Systems and Tools Weimin Teng, and Palantir Maintenance & Reliability Lead Daniel Herman.

Hundreds of millions of dollars in revenue can be unlocked with a reliable plant that is both safe and productive. Even a 1% improvement in overall operational availability can translate to a significant value. However, reliability in operating facilities can be impacted by many factors, including unforeseen environmental events, planned maintenance, plant turnarounds, and unplanned equipment failures. Timely analysis of such events is crucial to inform actionable plans that improve reliability. Millions of static and dynamic data points must be comprehensively analyzed to understand the potential impact of reliability-related vulnerabilities, risks, and bad actors. For bp, this includes the rapidly growing universe of engineering, transactional maintenance, and sensor data points generated every day across the globe.

The potential to deliver business insights from these complex data assets is enormous. The Palantir Foundry platform makes it possible with quicker access to connected data, low-code data transformation technology, and easy to use data visualization solutions and operational applications — all in one environment.

In this blog post, we explore how Palantir and bp — partners since 2014 — are embarking on a new undertaking to enable the transformation of bpSolutions’ reliability discipline. With operations in Europe, North and South America, Australasia, Asia, and Africa, bp is committed to delivering energy more safely, efficiently, and reliably — especially in times of disruption. Improving plant reliability is mission critical and even more challenging for one of the world’s largest integrated energy companies.

Palantir’s Foundry platform brings data to end users across multiple regions in a coherent fashion. In collaboration with reliability subject matter experts, data is transformed, organized, and analyzed to support informed decision-making across different levels of the organization.

How we started

Given the global nature of the discipline, deciding where to begin is a challenge. To find our starting point, we first focused on understanding existing datasets and their value and relevance to reliability performance.

By using the Foundry platform, we were able to connect existing data from disparate systems in a consistent manner, then organized the data to reveal bp production reliability and operational availability performance at regional, asset, choke, and systems levels. Displayed in a simple and easy to interpret format, this exercise yielded insights into where investments in digital transformation efforts could be beneficial to the business, and how to structure the underlying digital journey.

From standalone to a connected data network to insights

Eventually, every step of the business process becomes a coherent data point. While these individual points may produce limited meaning, when knitted together they create a story. Teams working on individual step(s) in the process have limited visibility into the big picture and are unable to track end-to-end execution effectively. By contrast, connecting data across disparate systems that support various reliability and availability analytics in a central, accessible location enables a common operating picture for the organization to assess the effectiveness of existing processes, identify pain points, and generate new insights.

For bp, our initial transformation effort mapped how key datasets interact with each other in the reliability ecosystem. Through the connected data network, users have the visibility necessary to identify points of friction, touch points between different business process, and opportunities to engage in cross-team collaboration.

Furthermore, this ground-up connectivity and data transparency improved alignment between leadership and front-line engineers and enabled a data-driven approach to delivering business results.

Beyond intuition

In order to make a difference to the business bottom line, intuition and business knowledge must be paired with data. Partnering with reliability subject matter experts and equipping them with data-driven tooling that sheds light on siloed data powers them in their day-to-day work and has many benefits. First, these experts are able to verify the accuracy, completeness, and relevance of the data. Second, examining insights surfaced by data can spark new ideas and thinking. For example, reviewing historic reliability and operational availability performance data and associated investigations, maintenance, turnaround activities and actions over several years, engineers can assess potential future impacts. Third, by automating trusted data preparation, engineers spend far less time collecting and manipulating data and more time on analytics.

The benefits of bringing together reliability subject matter experts with Foundry to generate business-influencing insights are evident in our use case with bpSolutions’ reliability discipline. We brought together bp engineers from both the central and regional teams to ask them for their perspectives and data needs. Once we provided greater visibility into the data via the Foundry platform, they proposed unique solutions to improve their day-to-day analytics efforts and opportunities to improve data quality emerged. This connected data network was built to scale and to enable a multitude of use cases simultaneously. It has freed up engineers to get more involved in decision making, resulting in positive business impact. This is just the start of bp’s digital transformation journey. We are currently working to extend the model to enable more business processes — from tracking reliability investigations coverage to identifying bad actors that may lead to production losses.

In summary, we hope this partnership reflection promotes understanding of where and how best to start a digital transformation journey in a complex global organization. It underscores our key takeaways: that data is a valuable asset and plays a key role in providing business insight across business processes. Palantir’s platform promotes cross-team collaboration. It enables a centralized and consistently built operational picture to bring leadership and front-line users closer to delivery of common goals and enhances day to day decision making.
