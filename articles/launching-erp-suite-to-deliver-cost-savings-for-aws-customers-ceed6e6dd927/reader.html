Launching ERP Suite to Deliver Cost Savings for AWS Customers

2021-03-05 · Original →

Enterprises across sectors invest heavily in Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems, and they share in a common struggle: how to realize the full value from disparate ERP data. To address this problem, Palantir developed the ERP Suite, a turnkey solution optimized to run on Amazon Web Services (AWS) that enables rapid integration and analysis of data within the ERP landscape. The ERP Suite is now available to all AWS customers, and has already been adopted by a number of enterprises, including bp and LANXESS, to enable significant cost savings within weeks.

The ERP Suite

For years, Palantir has been helping customers get more value out of their ERP estates. Aggregation of data from any platform, including existing data lakes, allows both engineers and non-technical users to leverage the full granularity of their ERP data to ask and answer business questions and execute data-driven operations, without the need of in-house ERP subject matter experts or outside consultants.

The ERP Suite comes out-of-the-box with a library of industry-focused use cases, such as supply chain optimization, that can be deployed in days. Palantir uses a Palantir Foundry data connector developed for ERP applications to improve data discovery, accelerate the time-to-value of ERP data, democratize workflow creation and drive faster business outcomes.

Optimized for AWS customers to enable rapid deployment

The ERP Suite has been optimized to operate on AWS. As a result, existing AWS customers can leverage the ERP Suite quickly and dynamically, to respond to their unique needs.

The ERP Suite in Action: bp and LANXESS

ERP Suite at bp

Multinational energy company bp was an early adopter of the ERP Suite. Within two weeks of installing the ERP Suite, bp was able to unlock approximately $50 million in working capital and has the potential to unlock further savings if applied across their worldwide supply chain.

ERP Suite at LANXESS

Global chemical company LANXESS has also adopted Palantir’s ERP Suite to derive more value from its ERP data. Within days of installing the ERP Suite, LANXESS had visibility into its end to end value chain across multiple ERPs and the capabilities to simulate decisions and run complex what-if scenarios. LANXESS can now estimate and react to changes in demand and proactively make data-driven adjustment decisions to optimize its inventory levels in real time and avoid costly overstock or shortages.
