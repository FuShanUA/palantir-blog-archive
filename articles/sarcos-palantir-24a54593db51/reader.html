Pushing the Future of AI and Robotics to the Edge

Palantir’s collaboration with Sarcos Robotics & Technology Corporation (Sarcos) is pushing the boundaries of modern edge operations by combining the strength of Sarcos robotic systems with the edge capabilities of Palantir software. Palantir is focused on helping Sarcos stream high volume sensor data from their robots in real-time, run complex AI algorithms at the edge, and continuously deploy software updates to their fleet at scale, no matter how remote or disconnected the environment.

As Sarcos deploys its two flagship robots — the Guardian® XO® and the Guardian® XTTM — to an increasing number of customer environments and industries worldwide, computing at the edge reliably and efficiently is key.

The Guardian XO robot is a full-body powered industrial exoskeleton, offered via Robot-as-a-Service (RaaS) lease, that multiplies worker productivity while mitigating occupational injury and fatigue. The Guardian XT robot is a teleoperated dexterous robotic system that extends human capability in complex industrial environments (like shipbuilding, power line maintenance or pipe inspection), reducing the need for humans to work in risky or dangerous environments.

Palantir’s Edge offerings can enhance the operations of Sarcos robots as follows:

Palantir Edge Streaming: With Edge Streaming, Sarcos knows exactly what is happening with its robots at all times. Sarcos can stream data from approximately 125 sensors on each robot to the Palantir Foundry platform in real-time, enabling high volume data integration and analysis, driving rapid iteration and product development cycles. Sarcos is able to parse data collected and pull only what is needed from the robots, which can reduce the costs and latency involved in shipping terabytes of data to the cloud.

Palantir Edge AI: Sarcos can integrate AI models fleet-wide across edge devices with Palantir Edge AI. Metadata from edge-deployed models flow back into the organization’s core AI/ML infrastructure, accelerating Sarcos’ work toward autonomy through model retraining, offering long-term, reliable maintenance of edge-deployed AI models. Users can seamlessly scale AI models across remote, disconnected edge nodes with real-time sensor, video and/or audio data.

Palantir Apollo for the Edge: Sarcos can scale its fleet of robots by enabling continuous deployment of software updates from the single Palantir Apollo control layer. Apollo for the Edge provides homogenous control of heterogenous environments, where edge device requirements may differ based on location or customer need. The Apollo platform helps manage Edge Streaming and Edge AI technology, ensuring all devices are operating with the latest updates at all times, anywhere in the world.

Sarcos is focused on creating the most productive, cost-effective workforce in the world and its unique approach to AI technology helps support that vision. Sarcos is developing AI algorithms to unlock a new level of efficiency and adaptability for its robots by modeling the way humans learn rather than relying on high volume training data and tedious repetition. Sarcos robotic systems can mimic the actions of the human operator, building up operations in stages with a “task library” approach that has the potential to transform commercial robotics by removing the need to reprogram a robot with each new business need.

Palantir’s Edge technology can extend this capability to robots in any environment with continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) of AI models all the way to the edge. Palantir Foundry can also help organizations transform internal business operations by optimizing shipment and coordination of hardware parts, avoiding production stalls and streamlining spend. Palantir aims to serve as the operating system for a growing number of companies seeking to automate their processes and workflows. Successful automation will continue to be a critical competitive advantage in the evolving industry 4.0 landscape, where interconnectivity and smart automation are paramount.

“What makes the Sarcos and Palantir relationship so unique is the fact that we’re really pushing that technology curve. It’s the advancement that [Sarcos is] making in AI, machine learning and cloud compute, and it’s [Foundry] that allows us to be able to run those models and really understand what’s happening with the robot real time.”

-Sarcos CEO Kiva Allgood

As Sarcos robots are distributed to a growing number of unstructured environments, operating seamlessly at the edge and progressing toward increased autonomy is crucial. Palantir’s software platforms and edge offerings directly address these needs and support Sarcos’s vision to revolutionize the workforce. Together, Palantir and Sarcos are augmenting human capability and charting the future of robotics and AI at the edge.
