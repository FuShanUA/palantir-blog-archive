Palantir’s Commitment to Compliance: ISO 9001

2022-05-31 · Original →

There is a common misconception that regulatory frameworks hinder companies from iterating at the speed necessary to succeed. At Palantir, we don’t have that option. Compliance is integral to our success. Palantir implements stringent compliance standards across some of the most highly regulated industries while addressing their most critical problems, without losing momentum.

Palantir is proud to announce we have successfully completed our ISO 9001 certification as of April 1, 2022.

ISO 9001 is defined as the international standard that specifies requirements for a Quality Management System (QMS). Organizations use the standard to demonstrate the ability to consistently provide products and/or services that meet customer and regulatory requirements.

Palantir pursued ISO 9001 to demonstrate our structure, planning, processes, and resources are aligned and compliant. While this accomplishment often comes by sacrificing speed and efficiency, we achieved compliance while maintaining the same product iteration velocity. This certification validates the processes that support our longstanding pursuit of continuous improvement and quality.

For a copy of Palantir’s ISO 9001 certificate, please visit our Safebase Trust and Security Portal. The portal can be used to explore Palantir’s security controls, accreditations, and compliance of our software products. For additional questions, please reach out to your Palantir representative.

There are three critical elements that enabled us to pursue and obtain ISO 9001 while maintaining our continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD) product delivery practices:

  • Customer focus. We send our engineers into the field to work directly with our customers — configuring our products, helping them integrate their data, optimizing their workflows, and producing operational results in weeks, not years. By establishing a true partnership, we help customers get the most out of our products and engineering expertise.

This partnership means we have the feedback loop already established to continue to improve the quality of our products. Because of this, we had the data and process we needed to align with the “Customer Focus” and “Engagement of People” ISO 9001 quality principals.

  • ISO 27001 Program. Palantir had already established an Information Security Management System (ISMS) through our ISO 27001 certification. The ISMS helps drive many critical components that relate to ISO 9001 — such as risk oversight, metrics to support measurement and monitoring, and a successful audit program. Palantir’s Technical Compliance team was able to leverage these processes to align with the “Process Approach” ISO 9001 quality principal.
  • Apollo. Apollo is the true champion for ISO 9001. Apollo manages and safely deploys our Gotham and Foundry platforms. Each platform is made up of hundreds of individual microservices, each owned by a development team that writes and releases product features independently. This approach allows us to roll out updates across services asynchronously, increasing feature velocity. However, this type of microservice architecture traditionally makes the deployment and quality management of the software more complex.

Apollo automates this complexity away. It is configured to understand what to upgrade, when to do it, and how. It upholds change management standards to ensure quality and availability while continuously deploying safe and verified services to our product fleet. Apollo enables Palantir to meet the “Engagement of People,” “Process Approach,” “Improvement,” and “Evidence-based Decision Making” ISO 9001 quality principles.

With Apollo, Palantir deploys a minimum of 80,000 updates each week to existing Foundry and Gotham customers, at no additional cost to them. This is driven by one centralized operations team.

Apollo’s assurance of strict quality and compliance while maintaining true continuous deployment keeps our customers, developers, and quality team happy.

Palantir is excited to continue expanding and growing our compliance program. Our QMS has grown into a mature process and achieving the ISO 9001:2015 certification is a strong step forward in creating a framework under which we can continue to sustain excellence and pursue further improvements.
