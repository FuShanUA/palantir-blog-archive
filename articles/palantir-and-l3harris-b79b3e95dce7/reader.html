Reindustrializing Defense Through AI-Powered Production

Editor’s Note: This is a joint blog post written by Palantir and L3Harris about our partnership to reindustrialize America’s defense industrial base.

Introduction

The challenge facing America’s defense industrial base is not just about speed — it’s about rebuilding the foundation that makes speed possible. Our nation’s ability to rapidly reindustrialize and accelerate capabilities to our warfighters is fundamental to maintaining American security in an era of renewed great power competition.

Since 2023, the partnership between Palantir and L3Harris has deepened to confront this reality head-on. L3Harris now sets the standard for driving software-defined production: bringing artificial intelligence (AI) to the factory floor, streamlining operations and supply chain management with Palantir’s Warp Speed operating system. Together, we’re delivering breakthrough integrated capabilities like TITAN, the US Army’s Tactical Intelligence Targeting Access Node.

This collaboration demonstrates that when defense and technology companies innovate together with clarity of purpose, we can begin to rebuild production capacity, illuminate hidden risks, and most importantly — get the best capabilities to America’s warfighters faster.

Supercharging L3Harris’ Production

At every level of the enterprise, the Palantir and L3Harris teams are partnering to drive production ramp-up with AI. Key areas of transformation include:

  • MRPSpeed for Night Vision Goggles. L3Harris’ night vision goggles business leverages the automated actions suggested by MRPSpeed, Palantir’s next-generation Material Requirements Planning (MRP) Engine, to resolve shortages that bottleneck production in real time. MRPSpeed’s dynamic scenario planning capabilities further enable the business to optimize executive-level decision-making around demand prioritization, throughput, and capacity.
  • AI-Enabled Supply Chain Resilience. Millions of shipping documents are processed with AI to create a high-fidelity Country of Origin data asset across L3Harris sites, enabling intelligent, risk-aware sourcing and tariff-aware supply chain forecasting.
  • BOM360 and ChangeOS. The Armed Overwatch program drives millions in savings leveraging BOM360 to identify and resolve excess material issues and discrepancies across engineering drawings, Manufacturing Bill of Materials (MBOMs), and Engineering Bill of Materials (EBOMs). ChangeOS streamlines configuration management for Armed Overwatch engineers, surfacing automated cost calculations and downstream impacts of proposed engineering changes.
  • AI-Powered Troubleshoot Assistant. AI-powered troubleshooting assistants across L3Harris’ tactical communications production lines drive reduction in troubleshoot cycle time by providing technicians with suggested resolution steps informed by historical data and institutional knowledge.

Products That Are Better Together

The partnership between Palantir and L3Harris extends from the factory floor to the battlefield. Through the TITAN program — Army’s next-generation ISR ground station that fuses L3Harris’ communication systems with Palantir’s software — we’re arming America’s warfighters with a decisive battlefield advantage.

Beyond TITAN, L3Harris’ WESCAM sensors integrated with Palantir’s Sensor Inference Platform (SIP) deliver precision when it matters most, allowing the warfighter to see farther and decide faster. By fusing L3Harris’ high-grade imaging with Palantir’s AI-enabled automation, operators gain split-second decision-making advantages that traditional optics can’t match. This integration reduces workload while flagging critical threats instantly and exemplifying L3Harris’ commitment as the Trusted Disruptor to forge innovative partnerships that deliver superior capabilities to warfighters.

Through Radio-as-a-Sensor technology, L3Harris’ resilient communications systems and Palantir’s Foundry platform fuse to form advanced intelligence-gathering weapons, ensuring every network connection becomes another set of eyes protecting American forces. Across projects powering the American defense industrial base, Palantir and L3Harris are jointly delivering the competitive advantage for the American warfighter.

What the Future Holds

L3Harris continues to push boundaries and expand impact beyond traditional markets, bringing advanced AI and digital transformation to international partners in the Middle East, extending visibility and speed advantages to strengthen allied capabilities and interoperability. Together, we are exploring ways to strengthen border defenses utilizing L3Harris’ fielded radios as sensors and integrating and automating battle management systems.

This is what modern defense partnership looks like: faster, smarter, and relentlessly focused on the mission. These projects, and the runway for continued success of the Palantir-L3Harris partnership, exemplify the shared commitment to get capabilities to the warfighter faster and secure America’s absolute competitive advantage.
