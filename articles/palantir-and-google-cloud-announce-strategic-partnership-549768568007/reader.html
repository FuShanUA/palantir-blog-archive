Palantir and Google Cloud Announce Strategic Partnership

2022-06-16 · Original →

Offering Commercial Customers Smarter Data-Driven Decision Making

Editor’s note: Read Google Cloud’s post and explore related content on their page.

As the world continues to increase in complexity, business success hinges on an ability to effectively manage data, while translating analysis into better decisions and outcomes. To enable data-driven transformation for our clients, Palantir and Google Cloud are excited to partner in providing commercial customers with an end-to-end data solution, turbocharging Palantir Foundry’s operational decision making with Google Cloud’s capabilities in analytics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. Our partnership with Google Cloud offers organizations a fast time-to-value bridge between technical experts, IT, and frontline business users, empowering them all to work with their data in more meaningful ways.

We’re partnering with Google Cloud on three key fronts:

  1. Palantir is delivering its Foundry platform for enterprise data management on Google Cloud. Customers will be able to quickly and easily deploy Foundry and take advantage of its features, while also benefiting from Google Cloud’s global, scalable, trusted and sustainable infrastructure. They can also deploy Foundry directly from the Google Cloud Marketplace.
  2. Foundry will integrate with Google Cloud products to empower people throughout an organization to better work with data. Foundry users can bring together their data in Google BigQuery with a variety of other sources, and run Google analytics as well as AI/ML capabilities to help drive more effective decision-making and faster business value.
  3. Google Cloud and Palantir are going to market in order to help solve key industry challenges for our customers, starting with retail, supply chain, and the financial services industry.

Bridging data and insights so you can achieve business value with speed

By using pre-built Palantir Foundry workflows that connect with Google Cloud native and industry solutions, users can have a complete data foundation for enabling smarter Enterprise AI within weeks. Existing Google Cloud customers can quickly get data into BigQuery by directly connecting to source systems using HyperAuto powered by SDDI. They can then utilize Foundry’s operational application and decisions framework to leverage the capabilities of BigQuery and VertexAI. Palantir Foundry’s out-of-the-box workflow and rules solution delivers immediate business value and leverages Google’s AI/ML capabilities to help make our Foundry workflows even smarter when it comes to solving complex business problems.

In taking away the technical backend complexities of data integration, we can empower frontline users to apply their own expertise and interact with their data more naturally, while activating it in real-time as they look to produce their best work. When problems do arise, using Foundry on Google Cloud enables organizations to collaborate dynamically — and again, in real time — in order to stay on top of their business by breaking down organizational silos that would otherwise prevent proper analysis.

Solving key industry challenges

To stay competitive in this new world of data, organizations should truly understand their end-customers. We’re not just excited about how this new partnership can improve knowledge of end-users, but also in their understanding of themselves, and where they reside within their wider industry landscape. Here are a few examples:

  • Retailers can implement Palantir’s Inventory Management Workflows alongside Google Cloud’s Recommendations, Forecast, and Demand AIs to better predict when popular items will go out of stock, and also adapt to disruptions in their supply chains.
  • Banks can better detect fraud and money laundering activities by collating transaction data, online activity, and fraud alert data in Foundry, while also deriving insights by bringing it into Google BigQuery and layering AI/ML models on the same data.
  • Grocers can better address the persistent problem of operational shrink by bringing together data on sales, inventory, promotions, and products utilizing Palantir Foundry on Google Cloud, helping them better reduce food waste.

By building use cases for common business challenges, Palantir and Google Cloud can accelerate digital transformation for customers and build the foundation they need to make mission critical decisions.

Better together

Organizational transformation rests on an ability to use data effectively for operational decision-making. This partnership will allow businesses to leverage Foundry’s capabilities in data-driven decision making and situational intelligence on Google Cloud’s trusted and secure infrastructure.

Customers interested in learning more about Palantir and Google Cloud’s joint solutions can visit palantir.com/google-cloud and cloud.google.com/solutions/palantir
