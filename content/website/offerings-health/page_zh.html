The Connected Lab

Foundry enables organizations to build a complete 360 degree view of the experimental, clinical and analytical work taking place across a laboratory or discovery group.

By integrating and harmonizing information across high-throughput experimental methodologies and data modalities, hypotheses can be tested more quickly, deeper insights can be uncovered, and scientists can collaborate together more effectively.

Preclinical Research: Foundry has dramatically accelerated the ability of researchers to carry out drug discovery workflows and to uncover novel repurposing opportunities by analyzing the full history of over a hundred million drug response curves generated over the last decade.

Drug Discovery: Integrate high-throughput screening and machine learning, in a highly secure, collaborative data environment.

Real World Evidence (RWE)

Foundry helps Real World Evidence organizations accelerate research with out-of-the-box capabilities including pre-configured pipelines to transform data sources into the OMOP Common Data Model, a plug-and-play governance framework for data access and study approval, rapid cohorting tools for nontechnical users, and sharing and management of code lists and other critical phenotype and outcome definitions.

Clinical Trial Harmonization

Foundry helps R&D organizations harmonize historical clinical trial data to create a launchpad for pooled analyses - unlocking significant value from these rich, high-quality sources of multimodal patient data. Once patients are comparable across trials, the harmonized data can be used to generate hypotheses about indication expansion, biomarker strategy, or disease progression

Clinical Trial Site Selection

The key processes involved in starting a clinical trial - protocol design, site selection, and patient recruitment - require coordination between a wide array of stakeholders both within a Pharmaceutical company and across its partners.

Foundry provides a common platform where data sources relevant to optimizing these can be centralized and analyzed together: proprietary data on eligible HCPs, 3rd party licensed data on sites, historic trial data, and licensed patient population data.

Cell Line Development

Efficient cell line development is a critical component in development timeline, but often slowed by data management and quality challenges. Foundry connects data from across the lab and throughout the clone selection process, ensuring full visibility for selection decisions and providing insight into operational inefficiencies.

Sales & Operations

Rapidly changing market conditions affect operations across a company, from field rep allocation to portfolio optimization. Palantir provides a common operating picture to ensure life science organizations move in sync from supply chain to sales.

Supply Chain Control Tower: For marketed therapeutics, Palantir can automatically surface supply chain disruptions to operational managers, including full visibility into the option space (alternative suppliers, routes, warehouses etc.) and possibility to simulate the consequences of the alternatives.

Sales force: Foundry allows Commercial leads to integrate transactional records, CRM data, 3rd party licensed data, and direct intel from field reps to provide 360-degree account visibility, then test & deploy models to guide channel strategy and predict demand.

Biomanufacturing

Global pharmaceutical manufacturers rely on Palantir Foundry to cultivate data connectivity, overcoming the challenges arising from operational and data silos.

A connected company opens the door to leveraging automated alerting and predictive AI to monitor operational efficiency, optimize yield, and predict deviations.

Product Components

The Palantir Foundry platform enables the world’s most critical institutions to generate alpha across their data systems, operations, and decisions.

Over the last decade, we have worked with leading life science organizations around the world and learned from their greatest challenges in managing biomedical data.

Each of the use cases described above shares a common set of challenges and we built Foundry specifically to address these, providing our clients with four key building blocks to accelerate their digital transformation initiatives.

↳ Make data usable

01 /

Foundry provides the connective tissue between specialized IT systems for biomedical data. Out-of-the-box connections for any data storage system ensure that data of all formats can easily be integrated into a single analysis platform.

  • Automated data integration in any format (e.g., HL7, FHIR, CDISC, genomic file formats such as BAM or VCF, high-content image files)

  • Open architecture and APIs for bi-directional connectivity to any system type and data formats (e.g., EHRs/EMRs)

Even when data is centralized, data standardization projects can take years. We’ve built Foundry Archetypes to cut this process down to weeks, for both real world data and historic clinical trials data.

  • Templated OMOP pipelines for common RWE data sources

  • Clinical Trial Harmonization Suite for pooling historic clinical trial data

↳ Protect data at all times

02 /

Healthcare institutions are trusted with the stewardship of highly sensitive data, and concerns around access, security, and management often prevent full use of this data.

Foundry gives our clients the tools to build a trusted research environment, with full transparency and controls over when, how, and why data is used in research.

  • The Purpose Based Access Control module gives governance administrators an out-of-the-box framework for configuring Foundry access controls to fit their unique policies.

  • Our focus on privacy and security extends to our hosting approach. Foundry also enables organizations to comply with various regulations and standards including GDPR, FISMA, HIPAA, and GxP.

All data in Palantir Foundry remains owned by the customer—never by Palantir.

↳ Facilitate collaborative analytics

03 /

Foundry helps accelerate studies by allowing researchers to use the tools they are comfortable with, while making it easier to collaborate and iterate.

  • Point-and-click cohorting tool enables experts of all technical ability to quickly iterate on inclusion & exclusion criteria to define a patient population for further study.

  • We’ve built Foundry to be as open as possible for data science. Foundry's native code workbook tool allows data scientists to jump between R, Python & SQL, and can easily be connected to RStudio & custom high performance compute clusters to develop with specialized bioinformatics tools.

  • Foundry’s Model Management Archetype provides a framework for data science collaboration: models from across the organization are registered in a central library, and collaborators can easily review the methods and training data used in development. No matter where a model is used, Foundry provides the management tools to track performance, control release management, and ensure good governance is maintained throughout its lifecycle.

↳ Promote knowledge sharing & reusability

04 /

Increasing collaboration between scientific groups is critical for accelerating discovery. With Foundry Knowledge Store, researchers can discover past projects and get a head start from the cohorts, datasets, or logic created by their colleagues.

  • Biostatisticians can easily template their code and promote it for platform publication, allowing their colleagues to leverage logic to kick start new analyses. Similarly, if clinicians define a disease or class of treatments with medical code lists, they can promote it for reuse across studies.

  • Reproducibility is a key challenge in science, particularly when studies span data sources and institution. Foundry helps address this issue with automatic provenance tracking. Any research result, whether a dataset, model or visualization, can be traced step-by-step back to the exact version of the raw data that was used to produce it.
