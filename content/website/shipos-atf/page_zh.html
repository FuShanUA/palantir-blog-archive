About the Program

The ShipOS American Tech Fellowship is part of the broader Navy-Palantir partnership to deploy AI-powered capabilities across two major shipbuilders, three public shipyards, and 100+ suppliers. Participants will gain privileged access to manufacturing-specific training content, dedicated office hours, and ongoing platform support to ensure successful implementation and ownership of critical use cases.

What You'll Learn

Foundry Platform

Build proficiency in Palantir Foundry from pipeline building and ontology construction to Workshop apps and AIP logic functions.

AI-Powered Tools

Leverage Palantir's Artificial Intelligence Platform to build predictive capabilities and automate complex manufacturing workflows.

Capstone Projects

Build use cases from scratch using your organization's data—from requirements to deployment.

Quality Management

Develop systems to track quality metrics, manage inspections, and maintain compliance across the production lifecycle.

Supply Chain Integration

Learn to incorporate supplier lead time analysis, orchestrate procurement processes, and create intelligent logistics workflows.

Program Structure

Our 8 week program is designed for working professionals with support from your employer.

Platform Fundamentals

Build core competency in Foundry through hands-on workshops covering data processing, ontology design, value types, shared properties, and interfaces. Choose between coding and no-code tracks based on your technical background.

Manufacturing Specialization

Deep dive into shipbuilding-specific workflows including scheduling primitives, Gantt chart dependencies, materials orchestration, and quality tracking tailored to Maritime Industrial Base operations.

Build Your Use Cases

Work directly with your organization's data to scope, design, and build use cases from scratch. Receive hands-on support during office hours while maintaining ownership of your solutions.
