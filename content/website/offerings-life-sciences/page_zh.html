Partner
Parexel
Overview

Parexel is among the world’s largest clinical research organizations (CROs). They run clinical trials that are essential for testing new medicine in human patients for their biopharma clients, to accelerate delivery of life-changing treatments to patients Historically, their process involved manually creating comprehensive submission packages for regulatory review. This was time-consuming and labor-intensive, typically involving ~1500 employees. Parexel sought to modernize this process to improve efficiency and reduce time to market while maintaining quality and traceability and keeping patient safety at the core.

Examples of Our Work Together

Centralized Study Ontology → Parexel uses Palantir AIP to ingest and process hundreds of pages of diversified inputs, from PDFs to Excel and text files, into a centralized study Ontology.

Automated Submission Processing → Parexel has automated the process of producing their submission deliverables, tabular data sets, annotated documents and reproducible code, by orchestrating a series of AI agents to handle components of this process, reducing the time required to prepare submission materials by over 50%, from 10-12 weeks to 3-4 weeks, facilitating quicker drug approval and market entry.

Data Mapping & Annotation → AI agents are utilized to annotate and map study data into standardized forms, maintaining a human in the loop to manually override any LLM decisions.

Increased Data Visibility → The system provides earlier access to standardized data, which helps accelerate downstream operations by producing better first-time quality data to support safety trends, signaling and medical reviews, enabling faster patient access to new drugs and improving overall healthcare outcomes.

Partner
Large Pharma
Overview

In a growing market, a large pharmaceutical company faced declining market share. Lacking a centralized data framework and relying on multiple different tools augmented with manual effort, the company looked to Palantir to create an AI-powered tool to transform sales operations.

Examples of Our Work Together

Customer Segmentation → Simplify the cohort generation process by using the Ontology to identify relevant groups of customers. Improve customer engagement by providing non-technical users with instant analytics to validate the cohort composition. Use LLMs to leverage unstructured data to identify relevant customers for a specific campaign.

Next Best Campaign → Determine the next best marketing campaign for a specific customer by evaluating their previous touchpoints, campaign history and prescribing behavior. Use LLMs to identify relevant content key messages most likely to resonate with the customer. Dynamically improve the model through an automatic feedback loop with digital engagement metrics.

Sales Co-Pilot → Leverage the full history of interaction with the customer to generate talking points for the next call while using AI-powered insights about customer preferences to customize the next action.

Resource Allocation → Model different resource allocation strategies to understand the downstream impacts on projected sales and cost.
