Solution Overview

TITAN offers a flexible and scalable ground station designed to modernize the U.S. Army with the latest deep sensing and sensor fusion capabilities.

Our solution integrates sensors, networks, and automation to increase operational tempo and reduce time from sensor to shooter.

Our modular and open software brings together data from multiple domains - enriched with AI and ML technology - to produce and deliver decision-quality targeting information to warfighters and reduce Soldier workflow burdens and cognitive load.
