Overview

Entity Resolution (ER) is a critical data management process that involves identifying and linking records across different data sources that refer to the same real-world entity, such as a person, organization, or product.

This process is essential for ensuring data consistency and accuracy, especially in environments where data is collected from multiple, disparate sources.

By resolving entities accurately, organizations can eliminate duplicate records, reduce errors, and enhance data-driven insights.

Palantir Entity Resolution

Palantir Entity Resolution provides continuous matching of millions of records from disconnected systems with end-to-end visibility into each step of the resolution process, enabling your organization to deploy an entity resolution and graphing solution to gain a de-duplicated data foundation of high trust. Palantir Foundry overcomes the challenges associated with entity resolution for massive datasets by using hashing methods and AI/ML models to improve speed with reduced computational spend. Fuzzy matching techniques further improve accuracy.
