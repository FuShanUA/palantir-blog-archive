Sustainment

The U.S. Navy needs solutions to better refuel, resupply, and repair forces across shipyards and while underway, down to the last tactical mile.

The information needed to deliver timely readiness outcomes currently lies scattered across a landscape of siloed legacy systems, impairing the efforts of planners, maintainers, and yard workers to meet their mission. Our software provides a holistic, integrated, and up-to-date view of enterprise data to support diverse workflows and help improve on-time delivery.

↳ Asset Visibility

Palantir’s solutions provide 360° visibility into the location, operational status, and availability of every resource across the naval enterprise — down to the individual parts, components, and systems stocked in each warehouse and aboard each vessel — to support sustainment imperatives.

↳ Availability Planning

Our software offers a cross-echelon unified environment where planners can collaboratively assess all current and planned resources against projected demand signals to help reduce availability delay, mitigate the unexpected, and boost on-time delivery.

Our software delivers insight into the full naval supply chain, from the forges of the national industrial base to forward deployed resupply operations. Strategists leverage our solutions for resilient logistics to assess competing supply chain strategies, plan for disruptions, and redistribute existing resources to where they are needed most — whether to an alternate shipyard or to an alternate theater.
