Foundry for Food and Beverage

Foundry is revolutionizing the food and beverage value chain. From end-to-end supply chain planning and dynamic inventory management to maximizing production yield and minimizing waste, Foundry streamlines and scales critical systems across the industry. Our platform helps enterprises enhance food safety and quality assurance, optimize cost of goods sold, conduct precise financial forecasting, and automate restaurant ordering and delivery.

Foundry enables food manufacturers, food service organizations, and quick service restaurants to elevate their operations and capture significant value. By integrating disparate data and building intelligent workflows that augment core processes, Palantir is transforming food and beverage businesses for the AI era.

Our work in food and beverage ensures that food and beverage providers are acting as efficiently as possible, in terms of time, resources, and costs, to nourish individuals around the world.

With an integrated, real-time view of operations, inventory, logistics, and supply chains, enterprises can streamline operations, optimize decision making, and drive enterprise success. With vast unknowns in the future of the industry, ranging from rising commodity costs, volatility, labor shortages, and increasingly complex networks, Palantir’s abilities to help enterprises leverage their data to run multiple scenarios/what-if analyses allow businesses to develop the agility to combat issues at their necessary velocity in a cost-efficient manner.

Aramark

Aramark is applying Foundry AIP to match point-of-sale products with similar names, achieving a 99% confidence level in 30% of initial matches which was then automated drastically reducing the need for manual interaction.

Through utilizing the Ontology, Aramark was able to link products from buy side to sell side, creating a full data lineage, enabling atomic-level detailed financial and consumer analysis with future work centered around the integration of unstructured data to better understand the voice of the customer, and the creation of a full digital twin to predict and simulate future operations.

AIPCon US Sep 24 | Aramark →

Wendys

Wendy’s Quality Supply Chain Co-op (QSCC) has developed applications with AlP, promoting improved supply chain management by proactively identifying and addressing product shortages.

Leveraging advanced analytics, the system integrates unstructured email correspondence with suppliers, provides insights into inventory changes, offers solutions for shortages, and streamlines communication and decision-making, ultimately enhancing supply efficiency and the customer experience at Wendy’s restaurants.

Beyond Meat

Beyond Meat's supply chain runs on Palantir AIP. Starting from inventory management to production planning and order fulfillment.

Using AIP Beyond Meat operations have driven $20 MM+ yearly savings and countless man hours. AIP connects Beyond Meat's data across 35+ data sources to build a unified inventory view that helps production planners understand what to order, how to rebalance inventory across the network and how to best sequence production. AIP also helps their customer experience team fulfill about 50% of their orders automatically ensuring that AI can take care of what's easy while humans are freed up to spend time on the more impactful decisions.
