Critical Missions, Limited Budgets

From safeguarding national security to protecting public health, government organizations serve our nation’s most critical missions.

Each of these vital agencies operates on a limited budget and must rapidly realign resources against urgent priorities, or risk mission failure.

However, making informed decisions is difficult when dealing with billions of data points, which are often codified in disparate formats and distributed across a web of siloed systems.

02 /

Case Study: Army Vantage Contract Management

Partner
Army Contracting Command
Problem-Space

Historically, it was difficult for ACC personnel to understand how much funding was on a given contract and when that funding would expire.

Answering these questions required analysts to query multiple, disconnected systems for large chunks of data and then piece it together from various sources.

Solution

Within six weeks, Army Vantage and ACC integrated data from several existing systems (including GFEBS, LMP, VCE, EDA, and FPDS) into a single cohesive data layer.

Now, with a click, contracting officers have immediate visibility into their complete contract list — sorted by probability of excess funds with the assistance of an integrated, third-party ML model.

Impact

↳ Within the first year, the Army successfully deobligated more than $3.3 billion in funding, versus ~$2.0-$2.6 billion in past years.

↳ ULO identifier tool now has 1,000+ users across several countries.

End-to-End Financial Solutions

Once established, an integrated foundation can power a multitude of financial workflows — from budgeting to auditing, and all the steps in-between.

Budgeting

Budget analysts must often rely on static financial reports, limiting their ability to manage risk and adjust budgets as priorities change.

With Palantir, analysts can proactively identify variance and intelligently reprogram funds, while seamlessly collaborating with internal and external stakeholders.

Auditing

As billions of transactions stream across existing financial systems, Palantir surfaces discrepancies at pace, highlighting unmatched transactions, unauthorized payments, and pushing notifications to analysts for corrective action.
