HyperAuto Impact Study

The Challenge

A global energy company uses Palantir to integrate more than 300 ERP datasets and flag attempts to buy materials that are already in inventory. With all ERP data in Palantir, an alerting workflow identifies surplus materials, and flags when material orders at one plant can be met with existing surplus elsewhere. This workflow and others allow the business to dramatically reduce inventory expenses and optimize working capital. Within weeks of deploying Palantir ERP Suite, the business identified opportunities to save over $50M in working capital.
