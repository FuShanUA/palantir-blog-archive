01 / Overview

Palantir was founded after 9/11 to help defend America and strengthen U.S. national security.

Early Days

From dispatching our first Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) in the early days of the Global War on Terror, Palantirians have served side-by-side with the American Warfighter to solve the toughest and most important problems. While much of Silicon Valley spent years debating whether to support national security missions, our values have never wavered — they've been rooted in protecting and empowering the American Warfighter since day one. We believe that our nation’s most advanced technical capabilities must be harnessed for the defense and prosperity of the United States and our allies.

Belief

We believe that the people who should be forging America’s AI future are those who have already demonstrated their commitment to America.

03 / ATF – Veterans✪

ATF-Veterans is a 12-week intensive training program on Foundry + AIP.

We take veterans with technical aptitude and teach them how to use Palantir’s tools to solve more problems faster and better than they could before.

What we’re looking for

We aren’t looking for just keyboard warriors; we’re looking for the technically inclined across all Military Occupational Specialties who can wield Palantir’s tools to solve real-world problems.

Whether someone is on Active Duty, in the Reserves, the National Guard, or has left military service (with an honorable discharge), we need them to forge the future of American AI.

Future opportunities

This is the perfect opportunity for someone who has the instincts and grit to succeed at Palantir, but who could use a period of dedicated study to level up their Foundry skills before being considered as a potential new hire. Successful graduates will be considered for a position at Palantir or placement with one of our customers. Most importantly, they will be equipped with the technical tools to help cement American AI dominance.

ATF-V launches a new cohort each quarter

04 / ATF – Mobilize✪

ATF—Mobilize is an accelerated training program that teaches those with technical backgrounds how to rapidly utilize Palantir’s Foundry & AIP tools.

ATF—Mobilize moves fast and doesn’t hold hands. It’s intended to rapidly mobilize America’s technically inclined, cleared workforce and immediately get them into the fight to support the American worker and warfighter.

What we’re looking for

Veterans transitioning/recently separated from the U.S. Armed Services with an active security clearance (especially, but not only keyboard warriors).

Technically-inclined civilians with experience working in the Defense Industrial Base (DIB)—with active security clearances.

ATF-Mobilize launches new cohorts on a regular basis
